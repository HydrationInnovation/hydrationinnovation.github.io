Hydration Innovation makes it easier than ever to mix up your favorite drinks on the go. Our reimagined dispensing lid and pre-formulated pods allow you to enjoy a fresh, delicious beverage anytime, anywhere—no mess, no hassle. Our pods are designed with sustainability in mind—they’re made from 100% recyclable aluminium, which can be recycled infinitely, helping to reduce plastic waste. By using powdered or concentrated ingredients instead of pre-mixed beverages in single-use bottles, we also cut down on transport emissions by eliminating the need to ship heavy bottles, mostly made up of water. Plus, our airtight pods keep the ingredients fresh without the need for preservatives or stabilizers. And because you mix your drink right when you’re ready to enjoy it, all the vitamins and minerals stay at their best. Hydration Innovation—fresh, convenient, and sustainable, every sip of the way. Let’s drink better.
