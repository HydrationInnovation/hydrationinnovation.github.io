Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 6, 2025

Welcome to Hydration Innovation! This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website (hydrationinnovation.github.io) and use our services, including signing up for our mailing list.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use our website:

  • Email Addresses: When you sign up for our mailing list, we collect your email address through a form powered by Formspree.
  • Automatically Collected Information: We may collect limited technical information, such as your IP address or browser type, through our third-party service provider, Formspree, when you submit the form.

We do not currently use cookies or similar tracking technologies on our website.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To send you updates, newsletters, and promotional materials related to Hydration Innovation via email.
  • To process your form submissions through Formspree, our third-party form provider.

3. How We Protect Your Information

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information. Your email addresses are stored securely by Formspree, which follows industry-standard security practices. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Third-Party Services

We use Formspree to process form submissions for our mailing list. Formspree may collect and store your email address and related metadata (e.g., submission timestamp, IP address) on our behalf. Formspree’s data practices are governed by its own Privacy Policy, which we encourage you to review.

5. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Unsubscribe: You can unsubscribe from our mailing list at any time by clicking the “unsubscribe” link in any email we send you.
  • Access, Correct, or Delete: You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information by contacting us at support@hydrationinnovation.com.

6. Data Retention

We retain your email address until you unsubscribe from our mailing list or request deletion of your data. If you unsubscribe, we will remove your email address from our mailing list promptly.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will notify you by posting the updated policy on our website. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at:

Email: support@hydrationinnovation.com
